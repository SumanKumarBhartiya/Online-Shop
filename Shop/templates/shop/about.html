Online Shop

Online Shop Pvt Ltd. is an Indian e-commerce company, based in Bengaluru, India. The company is focused on online sales of the product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, home essentials, groceries, and lifestyle products. The service competes primarily with Amazon's Indian subsidiary and domestic rival Snapdeal. As of October 2022, Online Shop held a 23.78% market share of India's e-commerce industry. Online Shop has a dominant position in the apparel segment and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones. In November 2022, American retail chain Walmart acquired a 72% controlling stake in Online Shop for US$4 billion, valuing Online Shop at around US$5 billion
